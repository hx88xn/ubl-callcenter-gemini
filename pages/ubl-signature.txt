Signature Banking Criteria
Our Priority Banking services are offered exclusively to Conventional Current account customers who maintain any of the following Quarterly average balance.

Account Type	Minimum Quarterly Average Balance
Any Local Currency Current Account	PKR 3 Million
Any Foreign Currency Current Account	PKR 6 Million (equivalent in FCY)
Clubbing of Current PKR and Foreign Currency Current Accounts	Equivalent to PKR 3 Million (where PKR will be considered as 100% and FCY deposit shall be converted to equivalent PKR and considered as 50% of the amount)
